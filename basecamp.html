Selected Work

1) Yellow Collectors

Yellow was a scooter and bike startup that had operations in many cities in Brazil. I joined them in the first month of operations as their first product designer.

Everything started with bikes and then scooters. A few months later, to escalate the scooter operation and increase inventory on the streets from ~300 to 1000+, the company needed a proof-of-concept that working with 3rd parties was viable in big Brazilian cities like São Paulo.

These people should collect the scooters with a low battery after 9 PM and take it to our warehouses.

Later was added the option to deploy charged scooters to appropriate spots early in the morning.

I designed the app screens, interactions, and proper instructions to get the work done.

It worked and then was rolled out to cities like Curitiba, Porto Alegre, Campinas, among others.

2) Yellow Guardians

After adding e-scooters, the job of the internal team changed and they needed new features to take care of new electric vehicles.

I visited the warehouses to better understand their job and designed new flows to turn scooters on and off, help them signal that any scooter was in-house, and start checklists, which were their main task.

The operators applauded this version when it was released! It was exactly what they needed to do their job quickly and hit their goals.

3) Rabbot Flow

Rabbot helps companies manage their fleets by digitizing and automating operations through its Saas platform and mobile app.

The most important part of the platform is the Flow page, which allows users to see what is happening with their vehicles.

I worked with the team to create new features, power up existing ones, and evolve its interface design that is being expanded to the entire product.

When you click any card, it shows relevant information to the operator to get to the next step of the flow. Create tasks, mention others, add to the discussion, or check what was done.

Input fields for consultation or quick edits according to what’s happening right now in the field.

See finished checklists to understand what already happened with that vehicle.

Also designed a new Conversations screen to help clients speak directly to their providers without all the back and forth that happens when they use standard messaging apps.

Every conversation is stored and can be reviewed later to give clients a transparent overview of the service provided.
